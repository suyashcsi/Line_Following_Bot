How to install:

1. Download the "arduino-1.8.5-windows.exe".
2. Install "arduino-1.8.5-windows.exe".
3. Open Arduino and click on the "File" option.
4. Under "File" dropdown menu click "Preferences" tab.
5. In "Preferences" dialog box click on the bottom most clickable address (like C:\Users\RISHABH\AppData\Local\Arduino15).

6. Download the "Arduino15.zip" from link provided.
7. Extract "Arduino15.zip" to "Arduino15" folder.
8. Copy all the files from "Arduino15" folder and paste to location you have opened earlier.

That's all!!
Now you can upload code on NodeMCU!!
